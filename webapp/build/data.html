The current system consist of flags placed in close distance around 50 meters. The flags colors are different around the world. For example, in Bulgaria there are three colors: green, yellow, and red. Green - you can swim (no danger), yellow - you are not allowed to go to far in the sea or use inflatables, red - swimming is forbiden.

Although the colors and their exact meaning is different there is one simularity everywhere - the change of the flag is eighter the lifeguard`s responsibility or someone specialised. He needs to recieve the data and the process is very slow. We can use led screens to show the color and update it automaticaly.

Also, we are streaming the data in our web app so everyone can see where is safe for a swim. That can be really helpfull for the tourists in the sea/ocean resorts. Many beaches are 'wild', so they don`t have that kind of system, and those who have it work untill 6pm after that everyone is in danger. "Smart buoy" is there for your safety 24/7.
